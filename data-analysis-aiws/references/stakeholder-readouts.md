# Stakeholder Readouts

Research outputs should help a stakeholder decide what to do next.

## Default readout shape

1. Observation
- what changed
- how large it appears to be
- where and when it is concentrated

2. Best current interpretation
- what explanation is most consistent with the evidence so far
- what still makes that interpretation uncertain

3. Caveats
- data freshness
- metric-definition assumptions
- unstable segments
- low-denominator risk

4. Recommended next checks or actions
- the fastest next validation
- the decision that should wait until validation is complete

## Language rules

- prefer direct language over dramatic language
- avoid causal wording unless the evidence supports it
- distinguish "we observed" from "we suspect"
- keep caveats visible, not buried

## Bad patterns to avoid

- polished certainty on weak evidence
- listing many charts without answering the question
- mixing extraction detail with interpretation detail
- hiding unresolved definition issues
