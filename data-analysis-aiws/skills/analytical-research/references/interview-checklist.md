# Interview Checklist

Questions to resolve before analysis starts.

## Decision context

- What decision must this work support?
- What action will be taken based on the result?

## Audience

- Who will consume this?
- What is their analytical sophistication?
- What deliverable format do they expect? (notebook only, notebook + presentation package, other)

## Prior beliefs

- What is already believed internally?
- What would be surprising?
- What would confirm existing plans?

## Definitions

- What is fixed (not open to challenge)?
- What is open (can be refined by evidence)?
- What is excluded from scope?

## Evidence standard

- What level of confidence is expected?
- Is directional evidence enough, or is statistical significance required?
- What would "good enough" look like?

## Constraints

- Timeline?
- Data access limitations?
- Sensitivities (teams, products, markets)?
- Prior context that must be considered?
